Vo Van Thanh Binh

CEO - Thanh Binh Mobile

Bachelor of Software Engineering in Computer Science, BTEC FPT
Master of Business Administration (MBA), Vietnam National University of Economics and Business (VNU-UEB)

Vo Van Thanh Binh, born in 24th May, 2004; is a Vietnamese entrepreneur and the CEO of Thanh Binh Mobile, a leading mobile technology company in Vietnam. He is a visionary leader with a passion for innovation and disruption. Under his leadership, Thanh Binh Mobile has become one of the fastest-growing companies in Vietnam, with a strong focus on research and development.

Organization

Established in 2004, Thanh Binh Mobile is a mobile device retail chain (mobile phones, tablets, laptops and accessories) with the No. 1 market share in Vietnam with more than 3,200 mobile device sales points.
